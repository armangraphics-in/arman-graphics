How to use:

  • Add Product: Click "Add Product" → Fill details → Upload images → Save
  • Edit Product: Click the "Edit" button on any product row
  • Delete Product: Click the red delete button (confirmation required)
  • Primary Image: The main product photo shown by default
  • Alternate Image: Shows on hover after 1.5s (e.g., card back side, installed view)
  • Offers: Check "Has Offer" to show an offer badge on the product card
  • View Changes: Click "View Site" to open the main website and see your changes
  • Reset: Click "Reset All" to restore default sample products
